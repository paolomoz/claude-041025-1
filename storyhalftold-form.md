# Share Your Story

Help raise awareness about metastatic breast cancer by sharing your personal experience. Your story can inspire others and contribute to a better understanding of life with MBC.

## Why Share Your Story?

Every story matters. Whether you are living with metastatic breast cancer, caring for someone with MBC, or working as a healthcare professional, your perspective is valuable. The Story Half Told project aims to amplify these voices and create meaningful connections within the MBC community.

## What to Include

Share as much or as little as you're comfortable with:

- Your journey and experiences
- Challenges you've faced
- Moments of hope and strength
- Insights you've gained
- Messages of support for others

**Please note:** Avoid including specific medical details such as treatment names, dosages, or detailed medical histories.
